Personal Profile

Elham Mutahar

A Qualified Doctor of Dermatology with 16 years of experience in diagnosing, treating, and managing a variety of skin, hair and nail conditions. Proficient in a variety of treatments and dermatological surgeries including laser therapy, excision of cutaneous cancer and moles, skin fillers, injecting Botox safely, electric dermo surgery (cautery), melanin transplant operation and platelet rich plasma (PRP) injections. High level of knowledge and experience in managing a team of experts and the coherent and successful management of an exceptionally busy hospital comprising of 9 departments. My education into dermatology has continued for 20 years and as any other qualified Doctors, I continue to upskill myself and embrace learning of new treatments. I have a caring and professional manner and I chose the field of dermatology to help people who suffer with physical afflictions.

Leadership

As a skilled team player and capable leader, I possess the ability to effectively lead multidisciplinary teams within the field of dermatology.

Diagnostic Proficiency

My professional capabilities include proficient problem-solving skills, effective decision-making skills, and advanced diagnostic skills in the field of dermatology, encompassing conditions such as eczema, psoriasis, acne vulgaris, skin lumps, and bumps.

Excellence

I have leveraged my resource management skills to benefit my patients. Surgical Skills

Experiences

Entreprenuer
